Religion

Finland has freedom of religion, so everyone can choose and practice their own religion or choose not to follow any. Most Finns are Christians, but for many it is more of a habit than anything else. Talking about religion is not a common daily topic as Finland is quite secular country. All religious communities in Finland are listed here.

Below are listed few of the communities within Turku area.

The Turku Cathedral International Congregation and its members represent a variety of churches and backgrounds In Turku area. Services are held in English and all baptized Christians are welcomed.

Islamic community of Turku in Facebook (Turun Islamilainen yhdyskunta) welcomes muslims to join their activities.

Jewish community (Turun juutalainen seurakunta) and Synagogue in Turku welcomes people to join their activities.
